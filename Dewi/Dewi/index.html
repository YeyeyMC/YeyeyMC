Who i am?

My name is Yeison Muñoz, I am a student at UPB (Universidad Pontificia Bolivariana) studying Digital Entertainment Design Engineering. I am 19 years old. I am in the sixth semester. I started my university career at the age of 16, and during my time there I have developed different skills in different fields of the digital entertainment industry. Before entering university I had a great passion for creating entertainment content, video games, 2D and 3D art, game design and so on, and now that I have been constantly learning for 3 years with my career and all my projects , I feel the need to show the world my potential and my aspirations regarding this beautiful field of video game development. I am characterized by being a person very open to knowledge, inquiry and the exploration of new learning. I am also someone who has great virtues, values and other things that make me who I am, a creative, sociable person, lover of teamwork, and innovation. Many people limit themselves by whether they know about a subject or not, but I am not part of that, my goal is to always be ready for new challenges, new difficulties, new knowledge, I am ready for everything that presents itself.
